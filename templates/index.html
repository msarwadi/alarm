Start Your Day Positively

It's more important than ever to be on time so that you can fit everything you need to do into the time you have time do it. Our device and companion web app allow you to start your day positively by making sure that you get out of bed on time.

Life is hard, and tiring. Sometimes it's hard to just get out of bed on time. To help you get out of bed first thing in the morning, and not turn off your alarm and go back to sleep (we've all been there), we created wake-up.club.

It only takes 3 steps to set everything up! First, mount our device to your wall and give it power. Then, press the button on the device to calibrate it. Finally, set your alarm on our companion web app.

The ultrasonic sensor on our device detects when you get out of bed and turns off its alarm. If you try and sneak back into bed after getting up, it'll catch you and turn the alarm back on!
